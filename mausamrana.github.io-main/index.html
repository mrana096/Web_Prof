About me

I am a graduate student at University of New Haven studying Analytics. I also work as a Data Analyst where I spend most of my time turning complex data into actionable insights by building dashboards and predictive models with Advanced Excel, SQL, R, Python, Power BI, Tableau, and Superset. I also work on the engineering side of things like setting up data pipelines with Airflow, building data warehouse models with dbt, managing PostgreSQL databases, and automating business processes with Power Automate. I use Docker, Git, and CI/CD to keep projects maintainable. On the side, I develop applications in C#, Java, and Angular, following Agile and SDLC best practices.
